Open Surge is a fun and fast 2D retro platformer inspired by old-school games, designed to empower creators to make their own games and unleash their creativity! Currently under development, available for Windows and Linux.

Why Open Surge

Do you love fast-paced 2D platformers?

We too! The 90's were awesome. But now we can go further. This is an age of creators, not just consumers.

Unleash your creativity!

Coding can be really fun once you know what it can do. The sky is the limit with SurgeScript!

Open Surge promotes the free software and the open source culture. Use, study, hack and share!
